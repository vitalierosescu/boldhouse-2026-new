FAQs

How do I become a member?

Browse our membership tiers, pick one that fits, and apply directly. We'll be in touch within 48 hours.

What's the difference between Flexi and Fixed?

Flexi is drop-in — come when you want, no commitment. Fixed gives you a dedicated desk, yours to keep.

Is the Resident membership open?

We keep Resident capacity small on purpose. Inquire and we'll let you know what's available.

Can I visit before committing?

Yes. Book a tour and we'll show you around.

Can I upgrade my membership tier?

Yes. Upgrades are always possible, subject to availability. Get in touch and we'll sort it.

Is there a minimum commitment?

Flexi has none — pay per visit. Fixed and Resident are monthly commitments. Minimum contract periods apply.

Do you offer team memberships?

For small teams, we can arrange multiple Fixed desks. Reach out at hello@boldhouse.space.

What happens if I want to cancel?

Fixed and Resident memberships require notice as stated in your membership agreement. Flexi has no cancellation — just stop coming.
