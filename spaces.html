[ The basement ]

Basement

Boldhouse is home to designers, architects, filmmakers, strategists, and builders. We don't take just anyone - not because we're precious, but because we know who's around you shapes what you make. This is the club.

FAQs

What spaces are available?

Drop-in desks, fixed desks, meeting rooms, and the Bold Basement event venue. Each serves a different kind of work.

How do I book a meeting room?

Via Archie, our member platform. Booking is available to all active members.

What is the Bold Basement?

200m² of flexible event space in the heart of Ghent. Seminars, screenings, shoots, workshops — it fits whatever you bring. Email hello@boldhouse.space to inquire.

Is the Basement available for non-members?

Yes. External rental is possible. Email us at hello@boldhouse.space for rates and availability.

What's included with every desk?

High-speed wifi, barista-grade coffee, printing, phone booths, and access to all common areas.

Do you offer day passes?

Yes, via a Flexi membership. Drop in when it suits you — no commitment, no minimum.

Is there 24/7 access?

Fixed and Resident members have 24/7 building access. Flexi members work during standard hours.

Where are you located?

Sint Baafsplein 10, in the heart of Ghent. Hard to miss.
