FAQs

How do I become a member?

Browse our membership tiers, pick one that fits, and apply directly. We'll be in touch within 48 hours.

Can I visit before committing?

Yes. Book a tour and we'll show you around.

Who are the members at Boldhouse?

Designers, architects, filmmakers, strategists, copywriters, developers. People who take their craft seriously. We curate the community — that's the point.

What's the difference between Flexi and Fixed?

Flexi is drop-in — come when you want, no commitment. Fixed gives you a dedicated desk, yours to keep.

Is the Resident membership open?

We keep Resident capacity small on purpose. Inquire and we'll let you know what's available.

Can I host an event at Boldhouse?

Yes. The Bold Basement is available for seminars, screenings, shoots, workshops, and more. Email us at hello@boldhouse.space.

Where are you located?

Sint Baafsplein 10, in the heart of Ghent. Hard to miss.

Is Boldhouse only for freelancers?

No. We have freelancers, founders, agencies, and small teams. If you make things worth talking about, you belong here.
